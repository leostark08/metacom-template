What We Approach

Metacom provides valuable resources and expertise in technical, business, and marketing services to meet our client’s needs.

Strategy Consultation Services
We start by gathering information about our client’s project idea, perform a market analysis to determine best technology options for the project at hand. After several discussions to refine the idea, we provide our client guidance on operational and technical approach for the long-term benefit of the project. We will collaborate with our client to implement and execute the strategies at each step.
Marketing Services

Marketing is a powerful tool for any business however for blockchain-related businesses, the market necessitates even more targeted marketing campaigns. Metacom will work with each client to understand their marketing needs and design a precise and powerful marketing plan to promote their project to reach their ROI. Marketing focuses on digital marketing and community building to promote the project.

Academy

We believe knowledge is power. 'Education is the passport to the future, for tomorrow belongs to those who prepare for it today.' - Malcolm X

Through a series of public/private seminars and training sessions, we will introduce the power of blockchain to people seeking to be part of the growing industry. As part of this service, we will provide scholarships/funds to nurture and assist future entrepreneurs of blockchain technology to start their own projects.

Raising Funds

Metacom seeks to invest in projects that meet our investment criteria. In addition, we have a network of partners with a variety of investment objectives that we can connect to our clients to assist them raise funds needed.

Technology Solutions

Blockchains are complicated and with never-ending daily information flow, the need to choose the right technology for a project to succeed is paramount. We are here to help. Our team has years of experience in blockchain technology. We can guide you through the complex but rewarding blockchain technology.
